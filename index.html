教學理念 Teaching Philosophy

在教學時我會拉近與學生的距離，不是那種死板板的「我教你聽」。

我自己以前成績也不是到很好，基本上都是靠自己摸索出來的。曾經微積分都看不懂，聽老師上課也聽不懂，最後是靠自己慢慢讀起來的。所以我特別能體會學數學不懂時的那種無助感。

正因為走過這段路，我更知道怎麼用學生聽得懂的方式來教學。

I believe in building a close, comfortable relationship with my students — not the rigid "I teach, you listen" style.

My own grades weren't always great. I figured things out on my own — there was a time when I couldn't understand calculus at all, even in class. I slowly taught myself and worked through it. That's why I truly understand the helplessness students feel when math doesn't click.

Having walked that path myself, I know how to explain things in a way students can actually understand.
